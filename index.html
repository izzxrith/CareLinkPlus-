How CareLink+ Works

From smartwatch to caregiver alert in under 2 seconds.

1

Wear the Device

Student wears a CareLink+ smartwatch that continuously monitors biometric signals.

2

AI Analyzes Data

On-device AI processes heart rate, gait patterns, and voice tone in real-time.

3

Risk Detected

The model identifies anomalies and predicts potential wandering or distress events.

4

Caregiver Alerted

Instant push notification sent to the mobile app and school dashboard within 2 seconds.
